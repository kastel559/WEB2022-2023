Americano

  • Serves: waiter
Ingredients Quantity

Description Caffè Americano Hokitika Cheese and Deli, A Caffè Americano from Hokitika, New Zealand Alternative names Long Black Type Beverage Region or state Italy or Latin America Main ingredients steaming hot water and espresso Media: Caffè Americano Caffè Americano (also known as Americano or American; Italian pronunciation: [kafˈfɛ ameriˈkaːno]; Spanish: café americano, lit.'American coffee') is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from, traditionally brewed coffee. Its strength varies with the number of shots of espresso and amount of water added. The name is also spelled with varying capitalization and use of diacritics: e.g., café americano.
